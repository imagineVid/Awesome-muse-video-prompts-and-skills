# X Discovery and Video Evidence Policy

X is a discovery surface, not a direct import source. Raw twitterapi.io results remain local and ignored.

## Search Matrix

Rotate `Muse Video prompt`, `Muse Video native audio`, `Muse Video camera`, `Muse Video motion`, `Muse Video dialogue`, `Muse Video ad`, and official account/thread queries. Search replies and quoted posts when the visible post points elsewhere for the prompt.

## Hard Gates

Every published case requires explicit Muse Video evidence, canonical attribution, reusable prompt text, playable video, one primary workflow, and enough detail to explain action, camera, timing, sound, continuity, or exact subject count. Reject still-image-only evidence, launch announcements, generic mentions, unrelated models, hidden prompts, and duplicate media.

## Publication Record

Keep source ID, publication date, creator, prompt provenance, model evidence, direct video URL, workflow, and curation notes. Editorial completion must be labeled; never present reconstructed language as a verbatim source prompt.
