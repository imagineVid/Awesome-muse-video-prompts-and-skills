# FAQ

## What is Muse Video?

Muse Video is Meta's forthcoming video-generation model, previewed in July 2026. It shares Muse Image's pretraining base and targets native audio, prompt adherence, visual fidelity, and temporal consistency.

Primary references:

- [Meta AI engineering overview](https://ai.meta.com/blog/introducing-muse-image-muse-video-msl/)
- [Meta product announcement](https://about.fb.com/news/2026/07/introducing-muse-image-meta-ai/)

## Can I use it now through a public API?

Meta says Muse Video is coming soon to creators and Meta AI. The launch material does not present a generally available public developer API.

## What are the known limitations?

Meta explicitly notes remaining gaps in audio-video synchronization and physically accurate fast motion. The collection keeps those limits visible rather than turning a preview into a finished-product claim.

## Why are official cases also prompt records?

Meta published reusable prompts beside several playable launch clips. They are stored as official cases for capability browsing and as prompt records for workflow grouping, with media-aware duplicate handling.

## Where can I adapt these briefs?

Product links open [ImagineVid's reference-to-video workflow](https://imaginevid.io/reference-video). They are creation workflow links, not a claim that ImagineVid currently serves Muse Video.
