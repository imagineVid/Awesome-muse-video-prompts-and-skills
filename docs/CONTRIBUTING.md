# Contributing a Muse Video Case

Muse Video remains a preview. A submission must prove the model, prompt, and moving result rather than merely mentioning the launch.

## Evidence Bundle

Provide the complete shot brief, canonical creator source, original attribution, direct playable video or trustworthy preview, input mode, explicit Muse Video evidence, and one primary workflow. Keep dialogue, timing, camera, audio, subject-count, and continuity constraints exactly when the source provides them.

## Review

Maintainers verify traceability, prompt integrity, production value, playable video evidence, workflow fit, and duplicate source/text/media. A single official post may contain several distinct clips; those records are unique only when their media URLs and prompts differ.

Do not invent a version number, public API, or general-availability status. Reconstructions must be labeled honestly and cannot replace a missing source video.

## Rights and Removal

Attribution remains attached to every case. Creators and rights holders may request correction or removal through an issue.
