# Muse Video Workflow Taxonomy

Classify each case by the production problem it demonstrates. Every prompt receives exactly one primary workflow.

## Workflow Families

### Physical Motion & Temporal Consistency
Weight, contact, acceleration, balance, and continuity over time are the main test.

### Animals, Counting & Subject Continuity
The clip must preserve animal identity, exact subject count, anatomy, and spatial relationships through motion.

### Native Audio, Dialogue & Foley
Dialogue, acting, ambience, music, or synchronized sound is essential to the result.

### Camera Language & Immersive POV
Camera path, framing, perspective, reveals, or first-person immersion carries the shot.

### Commercial Story Beats & Product Motion
A product or offer drives a planned sequence of beats and a deliberate finishing frame.

### Stylized Animation & Educational Motion
A defined medium, transformation rule, graphic motion system, or explanatory sequence is the reusable core.

Choose the category whose defining instruction would most change the result if removed.
